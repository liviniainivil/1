— About

Hi there!

I’m a multidisciplinary designer currently in my final year of the Digital Futures program at OCADU. The program focuses on design in the context of emerging technologies.

I'm constantly trying to expand and evolve my set of technical skills and apply them to equally varied methods of problem solving and understanding. I believe that novel solutions to both high and low level problems can be found through combinations of these processes and ideas.

When solving these problems, I'm interested in understanding the social implications of the designed solutions while still considering the needs of the market and value to the enterprise.

The variety of my design practice, from 3D illustration to UX, has allowed me to create work for a diverse set of brands such as Facebook, Google, J. Cole, Vice, Shopify and Kendrick Lamar. This past summer I was a product design intern at Facebook in Menlo Park, where I worked on the Core Growth team, specifically on new user experience and emerging markets.

Recently, I've been working on my skills in Unity to be able to design for AR, as well as trying to improve my understanding of vanilla JS, React, and D3.

In my spare time I like to listen to podcasts, read, help my friends with their music, make art and ride my bike.

I'm in my final year of my undergrad program and am currently looking for new grad positions in UX and visual design.
